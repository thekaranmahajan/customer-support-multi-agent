Refund and Return Policy

Customers may request a full refund within 14 calendar days of delivery if the product is defective, arrives damaged, or the wrong item was shipped.

For standard returns where the product is no longer needed, customers may request a refund within 7 calendar days of delivery as long as the item is unused and returned in its original packaging.

Refund requests should include the order number, a short reason for the request, and photo evidence when damage is involved.

Once a refund is approved, the finance team processes it within 5 business days. Bank settlement times may add another 3 to 5 business days before the customer sees the amount in their account.

Subscription charges are refundable only when the customer was billed in error or when a premium feature outage lasted more than 24 hours.
